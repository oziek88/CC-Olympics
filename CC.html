Welcome to Creativity Club Olympics!

"Umm.. What's Creativity Club??"

Well you see.. Creativity Club is a VCU Organization founded right here at VCU! We officially became a club during the Fall 2015 semester and have continued to grow ever since.

"Yea but.. What do you do?"

Here at the Creativity Club, we take part in a wide variety of different games and events. These range from improv to cooking, or even art to creative engineering. Every day at Creativity Club is an entirely different experience. Coming this month is our very first Creativity Club Olymics!

"Woahhhhh Olympics? Tell me more."

The 2016 Creativity Club Olympics is a two day event that begins Friday April 22nd from 4pm to 6pm (Pollak Building) and is followed Saturday April 23rd from 12pm to 2pm (grassy area behind Snead). There will be FOOD! As well as a wide selection of activities to compete in. However, you must participate as a team of two. So bring a friend or make a friend when you arrive. There will be a winning team for Friday, for Saturday, and overall. So if you can only make it on one day.. You can still be a winer!! There will be PRIZES! Top prize for the overall winner is a $25 gift card to Amazon! MONEYYY!

"Free?"

The Olympics are free for members. If you are not currently a member it costs $5 per team. So essentially $2.50 a person. If you're interested in becoming a member, you can always just like us at Facebook.com/creativityclubatvcu and we'll get you all hooked up!
